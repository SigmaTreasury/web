Sigma Treasury specializes in foreign currency settlement for card issuers and manages the execution and FX risk arising from international customer spend. Our platform allows issuers to reduce costs and with our FX rate guarantee issuers benefit from wholesale market rates without the operational overhead or FX Risk.

Our solution

The Sigma Treasury platform allows card issuers to settle in the merchants acquiring currency for international card holder FX spend however maintain the existing single currency card holder structure. This reduces the operational footprint for issuers as card holder facing processes remain untouched.

Sigma Treasury provides a service allowing issuers and acquirers to setup their settlement specifically taylored to their FX therefore reducing costs by sourcing FX at more competitive rates than those offered by the card networks. Sigma Treasury guarantees that its rates are never less competitive than card networks. This provides issuers and acquirers with piece of mind that their FX risk is limited.

Sigma Treasury not only supports issuers in the setup of the settlement process, but completely handles the procurement (trading) of FX, management and settlement of those respective positions with FX counterparties, and subsequent settlement with the card networks. The all-in-one service for issuers provides piece of mind of an end-to-end process outsourcing with limited upfront investment and foregoing the need for a dedicated Treasury FX operation to be setup by the issuer.
