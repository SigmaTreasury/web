Sigma Treasury manages FX risk arising from international card spend. It offers better exchange rates than Mastercard or Visa for both issuers and acquirers when settling card transactions in foreign currencies.

The Concept

Credit card networks (Mastercard and Visa) guarantee rates for 24 hours therefore an opportunity exists if an issuer/acquirer can source FX cheaper than what the card network uses for conversion into respective currency

Depending on currency pair, customers realize a cost saving by paying less to settle amounts with card network. For example the EUR/HUF 2017 historical daily spread between Mastercard and market rates was c. 55bps1

Issuer takes no FX risk as Sigma Treasury guarantees rates no worse than card networks (instead of trading with card network, issuer trades with Sigma Treasury)
